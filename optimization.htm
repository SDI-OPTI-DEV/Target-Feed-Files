APPROACH

Theres more to testing than reaching statistical significance.” At Search Discovery we leverage an evidence-based approach that leads to continuous learning. It’s all about using controlled experimentation to find the optimal way to talk to each of our clients’ usershow to optimize marketing tactics and how to guide product roadmaps that deliver measurable business results.

IDEATION

Repository of test ideas in hypothesis library (roadmap) prioritized and ranked by best opportunities.

EXPERIMENTATION

Create requirements and launch experiment or personalization experience after all quality control checks are completed.

ACTION

Act or make decisions based on the outcome of your experiment.

EVIDENCE

Leverage a learning library to house the insights and recommendations for future tests.
